About the Instructor

Hi, I'm Iljya Kalai, your sailing instructor. I have always enjoyed teaching and have hosted many sailors on my first boat, Smetana, with which I sailed from Italy to New Zealand via Brazil and the Panama Canal. My second boat, Manuel o Audaz, brought me down to the Canary Islands from Spain during the off-season. With my extensive sailing experience and passion for teaching, I look forward to guiding you through this enriching journey.

Our Values

Awareness

  • Sea-state: Understanding the conditions of the water and weather.
  • Boat state: Keeping track of the boat’s condition and performance.
  • Personal state: Monitoring your physical and mental well-being.

Interpretation

Sail Trim and Balance: Learn to interpret the sails’ behavior and adjust them for optimal performance.

Experimentation

  • Boat Handling: Discover the realm of possibilities in boat handling.
  • Key Indicators: Identify key indicators to guide your sailing decisions.
  • Trial and Error: Use experimentation as a tool to learn and improve.

Safety

Risk Management: Develop a framework to identify and mitigate risks for safe sailing.

Course Highlights

  • Mixed-Level Learning: Beginners benefit from the diverse perspectives of expert sailors, while experts are challenged to push beyond their limits with advanced techniques like blind pilotage and hands-free downwind sailing without an autopilot.
  • Hands-On Experience: Gain practical knowledge through hands-on training and real-time sailing experiences.
  • Personalized Instruction: Receive tailored guidance suited to your skill level and learning pace.
